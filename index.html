About Wolverine Hacks

Wolverine Hacks is a student-run middle-school hackathon in the Bay Area. Expect workshops, mentors, food, swag, and prizes — all with a friendly, beginner-first vibe. We encourage creativity: games, hardware, art, or anything you can prototype in a day.

Schedule

Final schedule will be posted soon. Tentative: check-in 8:30am, opening + workshops 9:00am, hacking starts 10:00am, demo/presentations in the evening. Whether we do a 12- or 24-hour format will be confirmed closer to the event.

FAQ

Who can participate?open to all students +

Students under 18 from middle and high school are welcome. You don't need prior experience — bring curiosity!

Do I need a team?team sizes & matchups +

You can come solo or with a team (recommended team size 1–4). We'll have a team-matching board for solo participants.

What's the cost?free event +

Wolverine Hacks is free thanks to sponsors. We'll provide food, swag, and prizes.

What should I bring?essentials +

Bring a charged laptop and charger, a notebook if you like, and any hardware you need. Optional: sleeping bag if it's a long event, but we'll let you know format soon.

Will there be mentors and workshops?help available +

Yes — mentors from local schools and industry will run short workshops throughout the day. We also have a help desk for debugging and ideas.

Are hardware projects allowed?bring devices +

Absolutely. Robotics, microcontrollers, and Internet-of-Things projects are all welcome — just bring what you need and any special chargers or adapters.

Photography / media release?photos & videos +

We may take photos or record short clips for promotion. If you prefer not to appear in media, contact us and we'll accommodate you.

Prizes and judging?what to expect +

Projects will be judged by a panel on creativity, impact, technical difficulty, and polish. We'll have multiple categories so beginners have a fair shot.

Accessibility & dietary needs?we try to help +

Tell us on the registration form about accessibility needs or dietary restrictions — we do our best to accommodate everyone.

Contact / volunteering?help out +

Want to volunteer or sponsor? Email dhiaan.n.d@gmail.com. We welcome mentors, judges, and volunteers.
