About the Hackathon

Wolverine Hacks is a student-led hackathon hosted at Thomas Russell Middle School, bringing together young innovators, coders, and creators for a day of building, learning, and fun. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned hacker, this event is designed to spark creativity and collaboration.

FAQ

Who can participate?
All ages under 18 and students of all skill levels!

Do I need a team?
You can join solo or form a team at the event.

What should I bring?
A laptop, charger, and your creativity!
