About the Hackathon

Wolverine Hacks is the first and largest hackathon run entirely by middle schoolers in the Bay Area. Join us this February for creativity, coding, good vibes, and collaboration at Thomas Russell Middle School. We can't wait to see your creations!

FAQ

Who can participate?+
All ages under 18 and students of all skill levels!
Do I need a team?+
Nope! You can come solo or form a team of up to 4 participants.
Is experience required?+
Not at all. We welcome total beginners and advanced coders alike!
What should I bring?+
Bring a laptop, charger, student ID, and a sleeping bag. Food will be provided.
How much does it cost?+
It's 100% free thanks to our amazing sponsors!
Will there be food?+
Yes! We’ll provide meals, snacks, and drinks throughout the event.
More questions?+
Email us at dhiaan.n.d@gmail.com and we'll get back to you quickly!
