About the Hackathon

Wolverine Hacks is the first and largest hackathon run entirely by middle schoolers in the Bay Area. Join us this February for a day of creativity, coding, and collaboration at Thomas Russell Middle School—where young minds build bold ideas.

FAQ

Who can participate?
All ages under 18 and students of all skill levels!

Do I need a team?
You can join solo or form a team at the event.

What should I bring?
A laptop, charger, and your creativity!

Why Sponsor Wolverine Hacks?

By sponsoring Wolverine Hacks, you're investing in the next generation of innovators. Your support helps us provide resources, prizes, food, and mentorship to young students exploring technology and entrepreneurship. Sponsors will be featured on our website, promotional materials, and during the event.
