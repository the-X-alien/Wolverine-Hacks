About the Hackathon

Wolverine Hacks is the first and largest hackathon run entirely by middle schoolers in the Bay Area. Join us this February for creativity, coding, good vibes, and collaboration at Thomas Russell Middle School. We can't wait to see your creations!

FAQ

Who can participate?+
All middle and high school students are welcome, regardless of prior experience!
Do I need a team?+
Nope! You can work solo or we’ll help you find teammates at the event.
Does it cost anything?+
No, Wolverine Hacks is completely free thanks to our sponsors.
What should I bring?+
Bring a laptop, charger, and a sleeping bag (required if staying overnight), plus anything else you need to hack comfortably.
Do I need prior coding experience?+
Not at all! We’ll have mentors and workshops for all skill levels.
Will food be provided?+
Yes! We’ll provide meals, snacks, and drinks throughout the event.
What can I build?+
Anything you want—apps, websites, games, or even hardware projects if you bring supplies!
How are projects judged?+
Judges will evaluate based on creativity, technical difficulty, design, and presentation.
What if I don’t finish my project?+
That’s totally okay! Hackathons are about learning and having fun, not just winning.
More questions?+
Feel free to email us at dhiaan.n.d@gmail.com.
