FAQ

Who can participate? +
Students under 18 from middle and junior high levels. Beginners and experienced coders are welcome.
Do I need a team? +
You can come solo or with a team. We run team-matching at check-in if you'd like help finding teammates.
Is there a cost? +
No — Wolverine Hacks is free to attend. Sponsors help cover food, prizes, and swag.
What should I bring? +
Bring a laptop, charger, and your ID. Sleeping bag is required for overnight projects — please pack one. Water bottle and personal snacks are helpful.
Will there be mentors? +
Yes — we have mentors and teachers available throughout the event to help with technical and project guidance.
Prizes & judging? +
Multiple prize categories (beginner, technical, creativity). Judges are staff, mentors, and guest volunteers. More details coming soon.
Accessibility & safety +
We welcome participants of all abilities. If you need accommodations, contact us at dhiaan.n.d@gmail.com.
