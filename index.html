About the Hackathon

Wolverine Hacks is the first and largest hackathon run entirely by middle schoolers in the Bay Area. Join us this February for creativity, coding, good vibes, and collaboration at Thomas Russell Middle School. We can't wait to see your creations!

FAQ

Who can participate?+
All ages under 18 and students of all skill levels!
Do I need a team?+
Nope! You can work solo or join a team at the event. We’ll help match you with others if you want.
How much does it cost?+
It’s completely free! We’ll provide food, swag, and prizes thanks to our sponsors.
What should I bring?+
Bring a laptop, charger, and creativity! Optional: sleeping bag, snacks, and a water bottle.
Do I need prior coding experience?+
Not at all. We’ll have workshops and mentors to help you learn new skills on the spot.
Will there be prizes?+
Yes! We’ll have categories for beginners, creativity, technical skill, and more.
